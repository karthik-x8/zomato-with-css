About Us

Zomato was founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah in Delhi, India. Originally known as "Foodiebay," the platform started as an online restaurant discovery service Over the years, Zomato has expanded its services globally. It operates in numerous countries, serving as a comprehensive food discovery and delivery platform. Like many food delivery platforms, Zomato faces challenges related to competition, delivery logistics, and regulatory compliance. The industry is dynamic, and companies in this space often adapt to changing market conditions. Zomato has built an active user community through its review and rating system. Users can contribute to the platform by sharing their dining experiences and recommendations. Technology and Innovation: Zomato employs technology to enhance user experience, including features like real-time order tracking, personalized recommendations, and in-app promotions. Acquisitions Zomato has engaged in acquisitions to strengthen its position in the market. For instance, it acquired the Indian operations of Uber Eats in 2020. Zomato has played a significant role in transforming the way people discover, choose, and order food, making it a prominent player in the online food services industry. It continues to evolve and expand its services to meet the changing demands of consumers
